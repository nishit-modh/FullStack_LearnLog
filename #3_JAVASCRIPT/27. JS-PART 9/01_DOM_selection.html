Bat Man

About

Bat Man is a superhero appearing in American comic books published by DC Comics.Batman was created by artist Bob Kane and debuted in the 27th issue of the comic book Detective Comics on March 30, 1939.His fights against Joker is very popular.He has been considered DC's flagship character since the 1990s.Batman is the alias of Bruce Wayne, a wealthy American playboy, philanthropist, and industrialist who resides in the fictional Gotham City.

Creation & Development

Batman, created in 1939 by Bob Kane and Bill Finger, first appeared in Detective Comics #27. Finger shaped Batman's dark design, Gotham City, and iconic villains. Inspired by Zorro, The Shadow, and da Vinci's sketches, Batman originally killed criminals before adopting his no-kill code. Unlike Superman, he relied on intellect, detective skills, gadgets, and fear, becoming a lasting cultural icon.
